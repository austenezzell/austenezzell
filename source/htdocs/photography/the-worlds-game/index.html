The World's Game

The World's Game collection began as an attempt to merge two of my favorite things; soccer and photography. When my wife and I set out on a four month trip through Asia, I tried to play a pick up soccer game in each city we visited. After each game, I took a photo. The more I played, the more rewarding the experience. It has become one of my favorite ways to experience new cultures and meet new people. The world's Game collection was featured in The Independant newspaper in the UK and on several design and photography blogs.
